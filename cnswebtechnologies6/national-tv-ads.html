National TV ads

The mass media named TV has developed an enormous forum for consumers, clients, and partners to build, communicate and deliver a message via advertisements. In 1941 a ten seconds advertisement made its debut in the world and from then marketing never had to look back again. National Television has a far greater goal to pursue the highest marketing honor. National TV channel is the most effective way for marketing as it broadcast not within a region, area, or country but throughout the world. Most advertisements are broadcasts between cast intervals. The TV coverage is carried by V.H.F. or very high-frequency band spectrum and U.H.F of ultra-high frequency band spectrum. Speaking of television advertisements on a national channel means the short casting made on the channel that is produced and paid by any company or individual to promote a strong message.

Types of the TV commercial

Tv commercial has made marketing one of the fastest marketing media. In dissecting the types of commercials, a national Tv runs can be divided into two types:

  • Sponsored Ads:

    Telecasts like film, story, or any play that is aired on television are aired by advertiser sponsorship. Adviser markets his business through sponsoring these telecasts.

  • Announcement Ads:

    This is the most common type of advertisement advertisers are interested in. This kind of advertisement aired between telecast breaks. Advertisers pay for the time his advertisement aired on television. These are the most economical flexible types of advertisement.

Cost

Creating an opportunity to add advertisements in national tv to cast worldwide marketing not only markets business to a company or individual but also brings back a lot of revenue for that channel. Casting an advertisement on National TV can be costly. There are two main costs of a TV commercial. They are:

  • Production

  • Distribution or broadcasting

Some factors that affect television commercial cost are as follows:

  • Time of the year:

    A certain period of the year may cost more than the usual time like any festival or political movement.

  • Time of the day:

    People after a long busy day come home and enjoy tv programs at night. So, advertisement casting at this time has more viewers creating more cost.

  • Airtime:

    Time limit for advertisement casting is a maximum 60sec. The less time advertisement consumes the less cost it gets.

  • Demand:

    In a break more the advertisement casts the more cost adds.
