DVC

What is DVC?


DVC is a digital visiting card with NFC technology. DVC instantly shares your contact, social & location details, ready to be saved in a phone's address book.

DVC allows you to change your contact details anytime & anywhere without reprinting your card. Just have one 1Card & you would never run out of business cards at critical moments.

DVC lets you keep track of when, where & with whom you shared your contact details. All this without a custom app!

How does it work?


  1. Buy DVC. Tap/Scan your 1Card to build your profile in ~3 mins.

  2. The person you are meeting taps/scans your 1Card & sees your contact details on his/her smartphone.

  3. The person you are meeting on-the-spot saves your contact details in his/her phone's address book or in a virtual rolodex.

How is it better?


Just DVC

DVC is a modern & smart business card. It lets you continue the etiquette of sharing business cards but is much more effective. Being a digital business card you just need one DVC!

Instant sharing


It takes just a few seconds to exchange paper visiting cards. But it takes a good amount of effort to actually use the information printed on the card. Ironically, today the cards are printed using digital technologies & the information they carry is mostly digital. The emailId, website, social media links & address information is consumed digitally. So, why can't the card itself be digital or shared digitally?

When you tap DVC on a NFC smartphone or scan the QR Code on DVC, your contact information is instantly transferred to the receiver's phone. Moreover your entire profile can be saved into the phone's addressbook! If a phone's addressbook is the holy grail of business cards, DVC excels in it!

All your details, Always up-to-date


DVC can share your name, company, designation, short bio, phone number, emailId, social media links, addresses & your photograph through a snazzy profile. The only thing that can't be changed in a DVC is your name. Everything else can be changed anytime, anywhere & as many times as you want just using your smartphone.

No App Needed


DVC doesn't need a specialized app either for creating, editing or sharing your profile. With a NFC smartphone you just need to tap DVC & the phone to initiate the transfer. With non NFC phones any general purpose QR Code reader is sufficient.

Sharing History


Not all of us are good at remembering the names of new people we meet. Curiously, it's not prevalent to have one's photograph on a paper business card. By not being able to recall we lose a connection & sometimes perhaps an opportunity.

DVC lets you know when, where & who accessed your DVC profile. Among other analytics, you can derive how many paper business cards you saved!
